Welcome to Ahnies Cuisine!!!

Ahnies Cuisine is an exquisite big kitchen that specializes in meeting the every catering needs of our wonderful clients, with the best natural ingredients under good quality control management while also paying attention to details. Our services cater for all types of events from corporate office parties, birthdays, bridal and baby showers, anniversaries, weddings ceremonies and other social gatherings. We offer a wide range of cake flavours and designs; from baking to decorating, right down to presentation and packaging you can be sure that alot of thoughts and efforts has been gone into the finished product no matter how big or small.

Our Catering services are sure to meet your desired expectations. With our experienced chefs you are sure to have the best of local, Continental, Chinese/Oriental dishes, cocktails, Smallchops and lots more... Ahnies cuisine also produces packaged products like Beans flour, Ogi flour, Cookies, Chin-chin, Cupcakes, Breadrolls etc which are available on request and also for daily sales.
